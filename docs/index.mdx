## What is Decepticon?

Decepticon is an AI-powered autonomous Red Team testing framework designed to help organizations conduct realistic security assessments.

## Why Decepticon?

Traditional security testing has limitations. Decepticon brings AI automation to Red Team operations:

- **Autonomous Planning** - AI agents devise attack strategies
- **Continuous Testing** - Long-running engagement simulation
- **Comprehensive Coverage** - Web, cloud, network, social engineering
- **Safe by Design** - Built-in Rules of Engagement enforcement
